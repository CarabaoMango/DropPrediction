About Us

Carabao Mango Fruit Drop Prediction

Welcome to the Carabao Mango Fruit Drop Prediction System! Our mission is to revolutionize mango farming by leveraging cutting-edge technology and data-driven solutions. We’ve developed a state-of-the-art drone vision system powered by a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) image classification model. This system predicts fruit drops in Carabao mangoes before they even hit the ground. By identifying ripe mangoes, we empower farmers to optimize their harvest timing. But we don’t stop there! Our system also incorporates a fruit fly trap mechanism. By detecting pests and diseases early, we prevent infestations and minimize fruit damage. This results in higher fruit quality and increased profitability for farmers. Make informed decisions about your harvests with ease. Our system aims to transform these challenges into opportunities for mango farmers. We envision a future where mango cultivation is not only sustainable but also highly profitable. By combining technology and pest control measures, we contribute to the overall growth of the agricultural industry. Thank you for joining us on this exciting journey! Together, let’s nurture healthy mango orchards and ensure bountiful harvests.
